About Us

About

Pixrys is built as a community-driven hub for Irys art, turning daily uploads into a living archive of creativity

We make discovery simple. Artists keep full ownership of their work while Pixrys organizes and showcases it in one place for everyone to explore.

Our focus is on community and visibility:

  • Every upload is indexed and easy to find.
  • Creators can share portfolios and connect with new audiences.
  • Collectors and fans get a clean gallery experience powered by data.

Pixrys isn't just a site, it's a gateway into the evolving world of Irys-powered art, designed for both creators and the community that supports them.
